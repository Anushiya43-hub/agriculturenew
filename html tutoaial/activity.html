ACTIVITY OF KVK

The ICAR Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) in Ariyalur, Tamil Nadu conducts a variety of activities to improve the agricultural economy of the region. These activities include:

  • On-farm testing: Assesses the performance of agricultural technologies in different farming systems
  • Demonstrations: Shows farmers how new technologies can improve production
  • Training Provides training to farmers, farm women, and rural youth
  • Awareness: Educates the public about new technologies through extension programs
  • Seed supply: Provides good quality seeds and planting materials
  • Livestock supply: Provides livestock, poultry, and fisheries breeds and products
  • Bio-product supply: Provides various bio-products to the farming community
  • Weather information: Provides weather information
  • Soil, water, and plant testing: Provides soil, water, and plant testing
  • Organic manure supply: Provides organic manure
